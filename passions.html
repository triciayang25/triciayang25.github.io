ART!

Drawing

I've done both traditional and digital drawing. I usually use Procreate for digital and often catch myself double tapping my paper only to realize... it's paper.

Wire Jewelry

I also make wire jewelry which is the typical jewelry you think of but no sautering (in my case) and just bending wire until it looks pretty.

FOOD!

Taiwanese

The most comfort food is my mom's cooking but I like to think she drew inspiration from Taiwanese/Chinese cuisine when immigrating to the US in the 1990s. My A-ma's (grandmother's) cooking is one of the most special flavors in the world, though.

Japanese

With extended family in Japan, I greatly enjoy my travels and visits to the beautiful country with some of the most delicious and eye-catching meals I've ever had.

Anything!

I'm a big eater and not picky so I enjoy trying new foods and learning about the story, history, or culture behind each dish.
